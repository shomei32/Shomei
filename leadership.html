Leadership

Shomei is focused on value-based sustainable deals where there is a differential in the intrinsic value of the asset and our purchase price.

Often, this intrinsic value comes from our unique power to strategically add value to investments that other groups cannot offer due to the decades of experience our partners have spent in capital markets and renewable energy development, as well as the strategic alliances we have formed within our target regions.

Irfan Verjee

Mr. Verjee is a seasoned portfolio manager with extensive expertise in emerging markets, technology component supply chains, and high-tech software solutions. Mr. Verjee began his career at Oppenheimer Capital before moving to one of the largest and longest operating hedge funds in the world, Kingdon Capital in NYC where he managed a nine-figure book and delivered outsize returns in the hundreds of millions for investors. Today Mr. Verjee is an active investor and advisor focusing on the intersection of energy storage, renewable energy generation, blockchain technology, and transportation. He resides NYC and Vancouver B.C and holds a bcom in finance and economics from McGill University.
